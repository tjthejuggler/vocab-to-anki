    	
revelation - communication by a divine or supernatural agency
mosque - a Muslim place of worship that usually has a minaret
hajj - a pilgrimage to Mecca that is a religious duty for Muslims
caliph - the civil and religious leader of a Muslim state
jihad - a holy struggle by a Muslim for a moral or political goal
bazaar - a sale of miscellany; often for charity
calligraphy	- beautiful handwriting
arabesque - an intricate ornament that interlaces simulated foliage
Bedouin	- a member of a nomadic tribe of Arabs, Located in the sahara desert, usually related to trading
Hejira - the flight of Muhammad from Mecca to Medina in 622 which marked the beginning of the Muslim era; the Muslim calendar begins in that year
imam - the person who leads prayers in a mosque
sultan - the ruler of a Muslim country
minaret	- a slender tower with balconies, especially on a mosque, Used to call Muslims for prayer
muezzin	- the Muslim official of a mosque who summons the faithful to prayer from a minaret five times a day
sharia - the code of law derived from the Koran and from the teachings and example of Mohammed
Muhammad - the Arab prophet who, according to Islam, was the last messenger of Allah (570-632)
Islam - the monotheistic religious system of Muslims
Muslim - a believer in or follower of Islam
Quran - the sacred writings of Islam revealed by God to the prophet Muhammad during his life at Mecca and Medina
Sunni - a member of one of the two main branches of orthodox Islam
Shi'ite	- a member of the branch of Islam that regards Ali as the legitimate successor to Mohammed and rejects the first three caliphs
Moor - one of the Muslim people of north Africa, Made up the majority of Muslims in Spain
adhan - muslim call to prayer
